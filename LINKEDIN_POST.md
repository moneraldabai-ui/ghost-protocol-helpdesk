**What if you could run a full enterprise help desk system... completely offline, on your own machine, for free?**

I just shipped Ghost Protocol — and I built every line of it myself.

For months, I watched IT teams struggle with overpriced ticketing systems that require cloud subscriptions, constant internet, and enterprise contracts just to track incidents.

So I built the alternative.

---

**Ghost Protocol** is a desktop-first incident management system that runs 100% offline on Windows. No cloud. No subscriptions. No vendor lock-in.

Here's what it does:

🎫 **Incident Management** — Full lifecycle from intake to resolution with RBAC
📊 **Live Operations Dashboard** — Real-time metrics and department load tracking
📚 **Knowledge Base** — Articles, categories, feedback system, PDF export
👥 **End Users & Departments** — Complete organizational structure management
🔒 **Audit Log** — Every action tracked for compliance and accountability
📈 **Reports** — Analytics with PDF and Excel export
💾 **Backup & Restore** — Your data stays yours, forever

And yes — it's **completely free and open source**.

---

⚡ **Built with:** Electron • React 18 • SQLite • Tailwind CSS • Framer Motion
